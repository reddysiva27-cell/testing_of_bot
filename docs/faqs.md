# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## 1. How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to **Settings → Security → Reset Password**.  
You will receive a confirmation link via your registered email. Password resets cannot be completed without email verification.

---

## 2. How do I apply for leave?
Employees must submit a leave request through the **HR Portal**.  
Once submitted, the request will be routed to your manager for approval. You will receive a notification once the status changes.

---

## 3. Who should I contact for HR queries?
For HR-related queries, please email **hr@company.com** or call extension **2201** during office hours.

---

## 4. Where can I find IT support?
IT support is available via **it-support@company.com** or extension **1234**.  
Support hours are Monday–Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST.

---

## 5. What is the escalation procedure?
If an issue is not resolved at your manager level, escalate to **HR**.  
If HR cannot resolve, escalate further to the **Head of Department**.
